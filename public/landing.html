The Reality

RBI requires quarterly stock audits.
Most of them still happen over WhatsApp.

Banks with CC/OD borrowers above ₹1 crore must conduct stock audits every quarter. The mandate is clear. The process has no standard tool — so everyone improvises with WhatsApp, Excel, and Word.

  • 👁

  • 📋
    CAs type every RBI report manually Every borrower name, account detail, stock figure, DP computation, observation — typed into a Word template. Two to four hours per audit, for formatting alone.
  • ⚠️
    DP errors slip through undetected Wrong margin percentage. Non-moving stock counted at par. Inflated debtor figures. Mistakes that overstate DP and increase the bank's credit exposure — caught too late.

How It Works

One connected workflow. Four steps.

Each party has their own role in the platform. No one re-enters anyone else's work. No information gets lost.

1

Bank Issues Digital Mandate

Branch manager creates the audit, selects a CA from the rated panel, and both CA and borrower are notified automatically. No letters. No calls.

2

Borrower Uploads Documents

Borrower logs in to their dedicated portal and uploads stock statements, debtors list, and insurance documents directly. No WhatsApp. No email attachments.

3

CA Reviews & Computes DP

CA reviews documents, fills the audit form, and the platform auto-calculates Drawing Power — stocks margin, debtors margin, creditor deduction, cap at sanctioned limit.

4

RBI Report Generated

One click produces a formatted, print-ready RBI stock audit report with complete DP computation. Bank reviews and files — done.

How Your DP Is Calculated
Transparent. Formula shown. Reproducible.
// Drawing Power Formula
Eligible Stocks
 = Stock Value × (1 − Margin %)
Eligible Debtors
 = Debtors ≤ 90 days × (1 − Margin %)
Less: Sundry Creditors
Drawing Power
 = Eligible Stocks + Eligible Debtors − Creditors
 Capped at: Sanctioned Limit

AuditFlow computes this automatically from your uploaded figures. The CA verifies before submission. Every number traces back to its source — no black-box calculations.
