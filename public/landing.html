The Problem

India's Stock Audits Are Running on WhatsApp and Hope

Every quarter, hundreds of bank branches coordinate stock audits over scattered email threads, WhatsApp groups, and hastily formatted Excel sheets — with no accountability and no standard process.

  • 📱
    No Single Source of Truth Mandates, documents, and follow-ups are scattered across WhatsApp, email, and phone — impossible to track or audit.
  • 📋
    CAs Re-Type Everything Manually Hours lost entering borrower stock figures into Word documents — with every typo a potential compliance risk.
  • ⏳
    4–8 Week Turnaround is Standard Banks wait weeks for final reports. During that window, credit decisions stall and fraud risk remains open.
  • ⚠️
    Drawing Power Goes Unchecked Without automated DP calculation, inflated stock statements slip through — exposing banks to significant credit risk.

How It Works

From Mandate to RBI Report — in 4 Steps

One connected workflow for every party. Zero information silos. Full audit trail at every step.

1

Bank Issues Mandate

Branch manager creates the audit mandate, selects a CA from the verified panel, and notifies the borrower — all in under 2 minutes.

2

Borrower Uploads Documents

Borrower securely uploads stock statements, debtors list, and supporting documents through their own portal. No WhatsApp. No email attachments.

3

CA Reviews with AI Assist

AI extracts figures from uploaded documents and pre-fills the audit form. CA verifies, adjusts margins, and flags any discrepancies.

4

Report Auto-Generated

One click produces a fully formatted, print-ready RBI-compliant audit report with DP computation. Bank receives, reviews, and files — done.
