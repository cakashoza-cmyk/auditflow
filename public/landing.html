How It Works

From Mandate to Report in 4 Simple Steps

One connected workflow for every party — no more information silos.

1

Bank Issues Mandate

Branch manager creates audit mandate, assigns a CA from the verified panel, and notifies the borrower — all in 2 minutes.

2

Borrower Submits Documents

Borrower uploads stock statements, debtors list, and financial docs through their secure portal. No email attachments, no lost files.

3

CA Reviews with AI Assist

AI extracts data from uploaded documents and pre-fills the audit form. CA verifies, computes Drawing Power, and flags discrepancies.

4

RBI Report Auto-Generated

One click generates a print-ready, RBI-format report with all computations. Bank downloads and files. Audit complete.
